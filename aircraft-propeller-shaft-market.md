<p><p>The aircraft propeller shaft market can be categorized into three main types: tapered, splined, and flanged. Tapered shafts feature a conical shape, providing a secure fit and efficient power transfer while minimizing stress concentration. Splined shafts have ridges allowing for precise alignment and torque transfer, enhancing performance and durability. Flanged shafts utilize a flat, circular interface for easy attachment and alignment, making installation more convenient. Each type is designed to meet specific requirements in aircraft propulsion systems, ensuring reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
