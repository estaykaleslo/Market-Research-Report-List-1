<p><p>The cationic aqueous polyurethane market features two primary types: carboxylic acid type polyurethane and sulfonic acid type polyurethane. Carboxylic acid type polyurethane is synthesized using carboxylic acids, enhancing dispersion and stability in water-based formulations, making it suitable for coatings and adhesives. Sulfonic acid type polyurethane, on the other hand, utilizes sulfonic acids, imparting excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, which is ideal for applications in textiles and automotive sectors. Both types offer eco-friendly alternatives to traditional polyurethanes.</p></p>
