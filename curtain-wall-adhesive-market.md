<p><ul><li>Acidic Adhesive</li><li>Neutral Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The curtain wall adhesive market is primarily divided into two types: acidic and neutral adhesives. Acidic adhesives, often based on silicone, exhibit strong bonding properties and are suitable for non-porous surfaces but may degrade over time due to environmental exposure. In contrast, neutral adhesives are more versatile, providing excellent adhesion to various substrates without the risk of corrosion, making them ideal for sensitive materials. Both types cater to the demands of construction projects, ensuring durable and effective curtain wall installations.</p></p>
