<p><ul><li>Focal ERG (fERG)</li><li>Multifocal ERG (mfERG)</li><li>Pattern ERG (pERG)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electroretinogram (ERG) system market includes various types: Focal ERG (fERG), Multifocal ERG (mfERG), and Pattern ERG (pERG). Focal ERG measures the electrical response of a small retinal area, making it suitable for localized assessments. Multifocal ERG captures responses from multiple retinal regions simultaneously, providing a comprehensive overview of retinal health. Pattern ERG assesses the response to patterned visual stimuli, often used to evaluate retinal diseases and optic pathway integrity. Each type plays a crucial role in diagnosing retinal conditions.</p></p>
