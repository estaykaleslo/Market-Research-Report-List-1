The market features two primary types: single-stage isolators, which offer basic isolation by allowing light to pass in one direction while blocking it in the opposite direction, and two-stage isolators, which provide enhanced performance by incorporating two isolating stages to further reduce back reflections and improve overall system reliability. Each type suits specific applications based on performance needs.</p></p>
