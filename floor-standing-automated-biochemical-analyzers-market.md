<p><p>Floor-standing automated biochemical analyzers are categorized into semi-automated and fully automated systems. Semi-automated analyzers require manual intervention for certain steps, such as sample preparation and verification, enhancing user control but limiting throughput. In contrast, fully automated analyzers streamline the entire process, from sample loading to result output, significantly increasing efficiency and reducing human error. Both types serve diverse laboratory needs, with semi-automated systems often suited for smaller laboratories while fully automated systems cater to high-volume testing environments.</p></p>
