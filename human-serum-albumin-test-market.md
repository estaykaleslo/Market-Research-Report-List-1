<p><p>The Human Serum Albumin Test market is categorized into two primary types: recombinant albumin and plasma-derived albumin. Recombinant albumin is produced through biotechnological processes, offering a consistent supply and reduced risk of disease transmission. In contrast, plasma-derived albumin is extracted from human blood plasma, providing natural properties but facing challenges such as limited availability and potential contamination. Both types are essential in clinical applications, including volumetric expansion and drug delivery, catering to diverse patient needs in healthcare settings.</p></p>
