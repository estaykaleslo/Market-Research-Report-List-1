<p><strong>The Infusion Therapy Needleless Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Fluid Displacement</li><li>Negative Fluid Displacement</li><li>Neutral Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infusion therapy needleless connectors are devices that facilitate safe and efficient intravenous access. The market is segmented into three main types: positive fluid displacement connectors, which actively push fluid into the catheter upon disconnection, minimizing the risk of contamination; negative fluid displacement connectors, which allow fluid to remain in the catheter when disconnected, potentially reducing the risk of backflow; and neutral displacement connectors, designed to maintain a balanced pressure, preventing blood reflux while ensuring efficient fluid delivery. These distinctions influence safety and efficacy in clinical settings.</p></p>
