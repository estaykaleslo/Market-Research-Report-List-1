<p><p>The internal tissue sealants market comprises various types including fibrin-based, collagen-based, protein-based, and synthetic sealants. Fibrin-based sealants mimic natural clotting processes and promote healing. Collagen-based sealants utilize natural collagen to enhance tissue adhesion and regeneration. Protein-based sealants are derived from natural proteins, aiding in tissue integration. Synthetic sealants are engineered materials that provide strong adhesion and flexibility, catering to diverse surgical needs. Each type is designed to improve surgical outcomes by promoting tissue recovery and reducing complications.</p></p>
