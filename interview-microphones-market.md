<p><p>The interview microphones market comprises various types, notably Lavalier and Shotgun microphones. Lavalier microphones are small, clip-on mics ideal for hands-free use during interviews, providing discreet and clear audio in various settings. In contrast, Shotgun microphones are designed to capture sound from a specific direction, minimizing background noise, making them suitable for on-location interviews or film shoots. Both types cater to different needs within the market, enhancing audio quality and user experience in interviews and media production.</p></p>
