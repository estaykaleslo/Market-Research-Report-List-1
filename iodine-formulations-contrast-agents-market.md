<p><ul><li>Inorganic Iodide</li><li>Organic Iodide</li><li>Iodized Oil and Fatty Acid Iodide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The iodine formulations contrast agents market comprises several types: inorganic iodides, organic iodides, iodized oil, and fatty acid iodides. Inorganic iodides are water-soluble and commonly used in various imaging techniques. Organic iodides, which are often non-ionic, are favored for their lower toxicity and improved patient comfort. Iodized oil serves as a specialized agent for specific imaging procedures, while fatty acid iodides are used in targeted therapies and diagnostic imaging due to their lipid solubility and unique properties.</p></p>
