<p><strong>The Liquid Helium Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>He I</li><li>He II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid helium market is typically divided into two main types: He I and He II. He I is the normal liquid helium that operates at temperatures above 1.2 K and is characterized by its low viscosity and non-superfluidity. In contrast, He II is the superfluid variant, existing below 2.17 K, exhibiting unique properties such as zero viscosity and the ability to flow through tiny capillaries. These distinctions cater to various applications in scientific research, cryogenics, and industrial processes.</p></p>
