<p><p>Non-invasive Positive Pressure Ventilators (NiPPV) are devices used to assist patients with respiratory issues without requiring invasive procedures. The market is primarily segmented into two types: CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) and BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) machines. CPAP machines provide a continuous stream of air to keep airways open, commonly used for sleep apnea. BiPAP machines offer two pressure settings for inhalation and exhalation, facilitating easier breathing for patients with more complex respiratory needs, such as COPD.</p></p>
