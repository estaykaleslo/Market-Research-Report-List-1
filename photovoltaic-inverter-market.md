<p><ul><li>String Inverter</li><li>Central Inverter</li><li>Microinverters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic inverter market features three primary types: string inverters, central inverters, and microinverters. String inverters connect multiple solar panels in series, making them cost-effective for residential systems but less efficient in partial shading situations. Central inverters are designed for large-scale solar farms, providing high efficiency and management for numerous panels. In contrast, microinverters are installed on individual panels, optimizing performance by allowing each module to operate independently, enhancing energy output in diverse conditions. Each type caters to different installation scales and site conditions.</p></p>
