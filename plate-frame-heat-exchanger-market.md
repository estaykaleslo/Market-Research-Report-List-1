<p><p>The Plate Frame Heat Exchanger market is primarily categorized into two types: Gasketed Plate and Frame Heat Exchangers and Welded Plate and Frame Heat Exchangers. Gasketed models feature removable plates held together by gaskets, allowing for easy maintenance and cleaning, making them ideal for applications with varying fluid properties. In contrast, Welded models consist of plates permanently joined together, offering higher pressure and temperature resistance, and are suited for demanding industrial applications where leakage prevention is critical.</p></p>
