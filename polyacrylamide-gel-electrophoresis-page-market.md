<p><p>Polyacrylamide Gel Electrophoresis (PAGE) is a laboratory technique used to separate macromolecules, primarily proteins and nucleic acids, based on their size and charge. This method employs a gel matrix made of polyacrylamide, which provides a medium for the migration of molecules when an electric current is applied. PAGE is widely used in various applications, including protein analysis, DNA fragmentation studies, and assessing the purity of biopharmaceuticals.</p><p>The Polyacrylamide Gel Electrophoresis (PAGE) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.2% during the forecast period.
