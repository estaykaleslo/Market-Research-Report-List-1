<p><ul><li>Tube Type</li><li>Rotary Type</li><li>Vibrating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable viscometer market comprises three main types: tube type, rotary type, and vibrating type. Tube type viscometers measure viscosity based on fluid flow through a tube, providing quick results. Rotary viscometers utilize a rotating spindle to assess viscosity, suitable for a wide range of substances. Vibrating viscometers determine viscosity using oscillation frequency changes, offering high accuracy for low volumes. Each type caters to specific applications across industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and petrochemicals, ensuring efficient fluid analysis.</p></p>
