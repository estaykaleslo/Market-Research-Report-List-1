<p><p>The printed woven bags market is primarily segmented into two types: laminated and non-laminated bags. Laminated woven bags are coated with a layer of plastic, enhancing their durability, moisture resistance, and print quality, making them ideal for branding and protection of contents. In contrast, non-laminated woven bags are more environmentally friendly and breathable but may offer less protection against moisture and wear. Both types cater to various industries, including agriculture, retail, and packaging, driven by consumer preferences and sustainability trends.</p></p>
