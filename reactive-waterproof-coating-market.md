<p><p>The reactive waterproof coating market encompasses two primary types: liquid reactive waterproof coatings and dry reactive waterproof coatings. Liquid reactive coatings are applied in a fluid state, forming a durable, impermeable barrier upon curing, ideal for seamless applications on various surfaces. In contrast, dry reactive coatings consist of powder formulations that activate upon mixing with water or other agents, offering versatility in application methods. Both types are designed to protect structures from moisture infiltration and enhance longevity in diverse environments.</p></p>
