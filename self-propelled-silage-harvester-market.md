<p><p>The self-propelled silage harvester market comprises three main types: side hanging type harvesters, knapsack type harvesters, and walking type harvesters. Side hanging type harvesters are typically mounted on vehicles for efficient, high-capacity harvesting. Knapsack type harvesters are portable, lightweight models, ideal for small-scale operations and precision work. Walking type harvesters are self-driven machines that allow operators to walk alongside, providing flexibility in various environments and enhancing maneuverability for harvesting tasks. Each type serves specific needs in silage production.</p></p>
