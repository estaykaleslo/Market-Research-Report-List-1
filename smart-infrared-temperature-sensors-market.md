<p><strong>The Smart Infrared Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>No-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Infrared Temperature Sensors market is categorized into two main types: Contact Type and No-Contact Type. Contact Type sensors require physical contact with the object to measure temperature accurately, often utilizing thermocouples or thermistors. In contrast, No-Contact Type sensors use infrared technology to detect thermal radiation emitted by an object, allowing for temperature measurement from a distance without any physical interaction. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, including healthcare, manufacturing, and HVAC systems, enhancing efficiency and safety.</p></p>
