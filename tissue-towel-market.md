<p><p>The tissue towel market encompasses various types, including roll towels, single-folded towels, and multi-folded towels. Roll towels are continuous sheets wound on a cardboard core, ideal for high-traffic areas, offering durability and convenience. Single-folded towels are flat and typically dispensed one at a time, promoting hygiene while minimizing waste. Multi-folded towels, often interleaved, provide an efficient way of dispensing, allowing users to grab multiple sheets easily. Each type caters to different consumer needs, balancing functionality and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
