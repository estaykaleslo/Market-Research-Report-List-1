<p><p>Waterproofing admixtures are essential in enhancing the water resistance of construction materials. The market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Crystalline Type**: This type forms crystals within the concrete matrix when exposed to water, effectively blocking water pathways.</p><p>2. **Pore Blocking Type**: These admixtures create a dense network that fills pores and voids, preventing water intrusion.</p><p>3. **Other Types**: This category includes various proprietary formulations and technologies that offer unique water-resistant properties for specific applications. Each type addresses different waterproofing needs in construction projects.</p></p>
